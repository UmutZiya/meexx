ESTA 2026 EXCELLENCE AWARDS HAVE FOUND THEIR WINNERS!

YMB member YILNAK won the award in the Education category.

One of the most prestigious lifting and heavy transport organizations in Europe, the ESTA Awards of Excellence 2026 was held in Munich, Germany, this year with the participation of 450 distinguished guests. In the event where awards were given in a total of 9 categories, groundbreaking projects in the sector, creative solutions and pioneering applications in the field of safety competed fiercely for the awards. We congratulate all finalists and winners and thank them for the value they added to freight engineering services!

Here are the ESTA Awards of Excellence-2026 Winners:

  • • Lattice Boom Cranes: Sarens
  • • Heavy Transport (Total Mass ≤ 120 tons): OL-TRANS
  • • Heavy Transport (Total Mass > 120 tons): Allelys
  • • Safety Category: Liebherr Mobile and Crawler Cranes – Werk Ehingen GmbH
  • • Innovation Award: Mammoet
  • •SPMT | SPT (Self-Propelled Modular Transporters): Wagenborg Nedlift
  • • Education Category: YILNAK
  • Winners: https://estaeurope.eu/2026-awards-winners/
  • Finalists: https://estaeurope.eu/awards/2026-awards-shortlist
