ABOUT MEEXX

We Meet in Bursa on December 3-6, 2025!

MEEXX – Machinery and Technologies Fair, the innovative platform for the machinery and technology sector, will be organized at the Bursa Fair Center from December 3-6, 2025, in collaboration with the Machinery Manufacturers Association (MİB) and KFA Fair Inc. This major event brings together numerous sectors under one roof, including metalworking technologies, sheet metal processing technologies, welding and cutting machines, machine tools, robotic systems, automation solutions, 3D printers, and industrial software.

By bringing together technologies at the heart of production, MEEXX offers the opportunity to showcase both the current transformation and the forward-looking vision of the industry. With its wide range from advanced manufacturing technologies to machine tools, from production lines to autonomous systems, MEEXX provides participants with new collaborations, strategic partnerships, and business opportunities.

MEEXX, which will host professional visitors from Europe, the Middle East, North Africa, and Asia in Bursa, directly brings together technology suppliers in the sector with companies seeking solutions. Take your place at MEEXX to stand out in global competition, position your brand in international markets, and keep pace with the industry!

What does machinery and technologies include?

Located at the heart of this ecosystem, the MEEXX Machinery and Technology Fair brings together all innovations in the sector under one roof, from metalworking technologies to sheet metal processing solutions, from machine tools to welding and cutting machines, from industrial software to digital production technologies.

The Future of Production is Shaped at MEEXX. MEEXX 2025 brings together companies at the heart of production technologies, innovative solutions, and a vision for the future. Smart factories, automation systems, robotic technologies, industrial software, energy efficiency solutions, and much more are showcased at MEEXX.

Throughout the fair, leading companies in the sector will present their latest products and systems; visitors will have the opportunity to experience technologies, deepen their knowledge with technical seminars, and establish strong connections through direct contact.

Realized in cooperation with the Machinery Exporters' Association (MIB) and KFA Fuarcılık A.Ş., MEEXX is not just an exhibition area; it is also a business platform where knowledge shaping the world of production is shared, experience is transferred, and professional networks are established.

OUR MISSION

To support the development of companies operating in the field of machinery and production technologies with a focus on digitalization, automation, sustainability, and innovation; to carry out cooperation, knowledge sharing, and representation activities to increase the sector's competitiveness in national and international markets.

OUR VISION

To be a leading platform that represents Turkey globally in the production technologies and machinery sector, guides innovation, supports sustainable production, and is recognized as a reference in industrial transformation.

Topics Covered by Machinery Technology:

  • Machining technologies (lathe, milling, CNC machine tools)
  • Sheet metal processing technologies (laser, plasma, press brakes, cutting-bending machines)
  • Welding technologies (gas shielded, TIG, MIG, spot welding systems)
  • Computer Aided Design and Manufacturing (CAD/CAM) solutions
  • Automation systems and robotic technologies
  • 3D printers and prototyping technologies
  • Hydraulic and pneumatic systems
  • Industrial software, data analytics and digital manufacturing solutions
  • Manufacturing and quality control equipment

Who is MEEXX For?

The MEEXX – Machinery and Technologies Fair brings together all industry professionals operating in fields such as machinery, metalworking, sheet metal working, welding technologies, automation, and robotic systems on a single platform. The fair attracts significant interest from industrialists seeking to improve their production processes, engineers keeping up with the latest technologies, investors planning to invest in the sector, entrepreneurs pursuing innovative ideas, and business decision-makers. It also offers an inspiring technology showcase for university students and academics, where they can familiarize themselves with advanced manufacturing technologies and develop a vision for the future.
